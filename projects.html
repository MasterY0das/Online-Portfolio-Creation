Dyslexacore 1

Dyslexacore is a groundbreaking project designed to empower individuals with dyslexia by honing their reading skills through targeted eye training. Leveraging state-of-the-art eye-tracking technology, the program offers interactive exercises tailored to improve specific eye movements vital for effective reading. These exercises, presented in engaging formats like games or puzzles, are personalized to each user's needs, dynamically adjusting difficulty levels as they progress. With real-time feedback and accessibility features such as adjustable text sizes and dyslexia-friendly fonts, Dyslexacore ensures inclusivity and effectiveness. By integrating seamlessly into educational programs, it provides educators with invaluable insights, fostering a supportive environment where dyslexic individuals can flourish in their reading abilities with confidence. .

Mind Up!

Our product mainly targets students but also extends to those in dire need of regulating their work time and breaks. Many high school students find themselves overloaded with work, leading to burnout and mental wellness issues that can affect their lives beyond school. This problem persists throughout life, as people often face stressful workloads without realizing how much they are pushing themselves. Our team aims to create a Google Chrome extension that can be easily installed to help manage daily tasks with regulated breaks and fun games to play during those breaks. Utilizing JavaScript, CSS, and HTML, we have developed a simple yet effective tool to assist with everyday tasks, easing the pressure on both students and adults. This extension includes a task manager where users can note their tasks, making it easier to manage a hectic schedule and save time by getting straight to what needs to be done.
